Selected Projects

Automated Depth Inspection Tool

Python-based tool that extracts depth metrics for specific variants and automatically flags problematic low-coverage regions.

Impact: Reduced depth verification time from 30+ minutes per sample to under 2 minutes. Eliminated manual IGV checks for routine cases.

Clinical Variant Inspection Pipeline

End-to-end automation for VCF processing: QC consolidation, annotation enrichment, allele balance filtering, and clinician-ready summaries.

Impact: Turned 3-4 hours of manual Excel work into a 5-minute automated process. Improved consistency and reduced human error.

SMN1/SMN2 CNV Caller

Depth-based copy number estimation for the SMN locus—one of genomics' most challenging regions due to 99.9% sequence homology.

Impact: Enabled pre-screening for SMA carrier status in routine WGS workflows, providing preliminary CNV calls for downstream confirmation.

Sarek Pipeline Web Interface

Custom web-based viewer for Sarek/Nextflow pipeline outputs, making QC metrics and variant results accessible to non-bioinformaticians.

Impact: Democratized pipeline access across the lab. Technicians and clinicians can now review results without command-line expertise.

HPO-Based Variant Prioritization (In Development)

Integrates Human Phenotype Ontology terms to score and rank variants by clinical relevance, combining phenotype similarity with population frequency and pathogenicity.

Goal: Reduce time-to-diagnosis by surfacing the most relevant variants first, with full QC context for rapid clinical interpretation.
